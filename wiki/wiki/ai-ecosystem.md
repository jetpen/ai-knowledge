# AI Ecosystem

The AI Ecosystem refers to the interconnected network of AI applications, agents, tools, data sources, and communication protocols that enable collaborative intelligence.

## Key Layers
- **[[ag-ui]]**: Connects agents to users.
- **[[model-context-protocol]]**: Connects AI to external tools and data.
- **[[a2a]]**: Defines how agents communicate with each other.
- **[[agent-network-protocol]]**: A broad collaboration network for intelligent agents.

## Evolution
The ecosystem is evolving from fragmented, isolated frameworks (like LangChain or BeeAI) toward open, standardized interoperability through protocols like MCP and A2A.
