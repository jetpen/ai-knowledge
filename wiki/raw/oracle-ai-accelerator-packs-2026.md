# Source: Oracle AI Accelerator Packs
URL: https://www.oracle.com/artificial-intelligence/ai-accelerator-packs/
Date: 2026-04-13

Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) AI Accelerator Packs are ready-to-use, full-stack AI solutions designed for specific enterprise use cases. They aim to reduce the time to deploy AI workloads from months to hours by automating the infrastructure and application stack provisioning.

## Key Features
- **One-click deployment:** Accessible via OCI console.
- **Full-stack automation:** Provisions networking, security, compute, storage, OCI AI services, and AI models automatically.
- **Targeted use cases:** Pre-configured for common business problems.
- **Flexibility:** Month-by-month access.

## Available Accelerator Packs
- **GenAI search chat agent:** Intelligent interface for context-aware responses, aimed at internal/external queries.
- **Delivery route optimizer:** Uses real-time data to optimize routes, reduce fuel/transit time.
- **Flagging explicit content:** Multimodal capabilities for compliance/safety in video content.

## Benefits
- Shortens path from launch to outcome.
- Reduces complexity of platform assembly.
- Provides a trusted, integrated ecosystem on OCI.
