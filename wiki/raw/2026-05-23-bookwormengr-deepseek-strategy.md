# DeepSeek's 10 trillion USD grand strategy

**Author:** @bookwormengr
**Source:** [X](https://x.com/i/status/2057909493250539891)

## Summary
DeepSeek is pursuing a long-term strategy that differentiates it from competitors focusing on immediate applications or incremental model improvements. While seemingly counterintuitive given their commitment to open source and lack of a traditional product suite, CEO Liang Wenfeng's approach positions them to potentially achieve a $1T valuation and contribute to a $10T industry.

## Key Observations
DeepSeek's path—referred to as "DeepSeek's Hero's Journey"—includes:

- **Mixture of Expert (MoE) focus:** Prioritizing hard-to-train MoE models over dense alternatives.
- **Algorithm innovation:** Inventing GRPO to replace the expensive PPO algorithm for Reinforcement Learning (RL).
- **Reasoning enhancement:** Utilizing Reinforcement Learning from Verified Rewards (RLVR).
- **Speculative Decoding/MTP:** Implementing Multi Token Prediction to densify training signals.
- **Infrastructure efficiency:** Mastering "ZERO bubble" pipelines to optimize GPU utilization.
- **Expert Load balancer:** Simplifying the deployment of MoE models, specifically through the "Wide Expert Parallel" strategy.
- **KV Cache optimization:** Inventing MLA, DSA, CSA, and HCA to maintain computational efficiency regardless of context length.
