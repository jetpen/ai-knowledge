Spec-Driven Development (SDD) thread from AlphaSignal AI.
- Central thesis: SDD is the new default for AI coding, transitioning from "vibe coding" to structured, spec-led development due to the volume of AI-generated output.
- Key references: GitHub (Spec Kit), Martin Fowler, Thoughtworks, Amazon (Kiro).
- Critique: Not a revolution, but a necessary discipline evolution from BDD in an AI-native code generation context.
