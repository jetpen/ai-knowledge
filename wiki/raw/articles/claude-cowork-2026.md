# Claude Cowork

**Delegate to Claude, delight in the result.**

Hand off a task, get a polished deliverable. Spend less time finding, formatting, and fixing with Claude Cowork.

## Key Capabilities
- **Scheduled tasks**: Automate recurring workflows (e.g., daily briefings, weekly metric pulls).
- **Cross-platform**: Works across Slack, Notion, GitHub, CRM, Linear, etc.
- **Human-in-the-loop**: Claude shows the plan, waits for approval, and allows redirection/refinement at any step.
- **Desktop/Phone access**: Works using specialized connectors or by interacting with apps directly.

## Industry Applications
- **Administrative**: Daily briefings, surface priorities.
- **Research & Analysis**: Size markets, professional deliverables.
- **Sales & GTM**: Aggregate feedback, identify patterns.
- **Legal & Compliance**: Organize legal documents for audits.

## Control & Security
- User-defined access permissions (folders, connectors).
- Human-in-the-loop approval before significant actions.
