# AMI Labs Secures $1.03B for World Model Development

TechCrunch report on AMI Labs, a research venture dedicated to building World Models using JEPA architecture, co-founded by Yann LeCun.

## Key Takeaways
- **Objective:** Move beyond LLM-based language learning to physical world understanding.
- **Technology:** Centered on JEPA (Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture).
- **Scale:** $1.03B raised at $3.5B valuation.
- **Strategy:** Long-term fundamental research over short-cycle product releases; open science commitment.
- **Infrastructure:** Massive investment focused on compute and talent across Paris, New York, Montreal, and Singapore.

Source: [Yann LeCun's AMI Labs raises $1.03B to build world models | TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/09/yann-lecuns-ami-labs-raises-1-03-billion-to-build-world-models/)
