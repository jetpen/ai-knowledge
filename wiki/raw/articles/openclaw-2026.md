# OpenClaw - Personal AI Assistant

**The AI that actually does things.**

OpenClaw is a personal AI assistant that performs tasks like clearing your inbox, sending emails, managing your calendar, and checking you in for flights—all from chat apps like WhatsApp and Telegram.

## Key Capabilities
- **24/7 Agentic Agent**: Self-improving codebase, persistent memory, and persona onboarding.
- **Platform Agnostic**: Works naturally in chat apps (Telegram, WhatsApp, etc.).
- **Proactive Automation**: Built-in support for cron jobs, reminders, background tasks.
- **Full Computer Access**: Can control files, Gmail, calendars, and other tools as if a human were at the keyboard.
- **Developer-Friendly**: Open-source, local-first context and skills.

## Connections to Hermes
OpenClaw is a key ancestor/predecessor project to Hermes Agent; Hermes includes an `openclaw-migration` skill to help users migrate their settings, tasks, and memories.
