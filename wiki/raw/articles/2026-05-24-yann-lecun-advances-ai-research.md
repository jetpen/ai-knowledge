# Yann LeCun's Vision for Advancing AI Research

Yann LeCun's AI research strategy is centered on developing architectures that move beyond scaling language models, specifically focusing on World Models and Joint Embedding Predictive Architectures (JEPA).

## Key Components
- **World Models:** Predictive internal models that allow agents to simulate, plan, and understand causality.
- **JEPA:** A predictive architecture that maps inputs to abstract latent spaces, training the system to predict how representations change over time rather than pixel-perfect reconstruction.
- **Modular Autonomy:** A modular architecture comprising a Configurator, Perception, World Model, Cost Module, Actor, and Short-term Memory.

## Vision and Strategy
- **Common Sense:** Learned through self-supervised observation and exploration, not just massive data labeling.
- **Hierarchical Planning:** Stacking JEPAs to manage hierarchical subgoals ranging from Millisecond motor control to long-term task objectives.
- **Open Science:** Commitment to collaborative research across cognitive science, neuroscience, and AI to solve the problem of autonomous intelligent agents.
