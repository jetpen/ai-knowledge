# OCI AI Accelerator Packs

Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) [[ai-accelerator-packs]] are ready-to-use, full-stack AI solutions designed to streamline the deployment of AI workloads on [[oracle-cloud-infrastructure]]. They focus on reducing complex platform assembly from months to hours by providing end-to-end, one-click automated provisioning via the OCI console.

## Overview
These packs combine [[oci-ai-services]], compute, storage, and curated open-source software to address common business problems. They automatically provision required networking, security policies, observability layers, and specific [[ai-models]] tailored for high-quality results.

## Key Accelerator Packs
- **[[genai-search-chat-agent]]**: Provides context-aware, LLM-powered search interfaces for enterprise knowledge management.
- **[[delivery-route-optimizer]]**: Uses real-time data to automate and improve logistics and route planning.
- **[[explicit-content-flagging]]**: Employs multimodal capabilities for safety, compliance, and regulatory adherence in video assets.

## Strategic Value
- **Accelerated Time-to-Value**: Enables rapid deployment of AI-driven use cases.
- **Low-touch Deployment**: Simplifies infrastructure management by automating the full stack.
- **Flexibility**: Offers month-to-month access to help enterprises demonstrate value without long-term commitments.
- **Ecosystem Integration**: Built upon the trusted foundation of [[oracle-cloud-infrastructure]].
