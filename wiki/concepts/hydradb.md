# HydraDB

Working-context infrastructure specifically engineered to support the formation and coherence of a [[Company Brain]].

## Overview
HydraDB provides the technical foundations for managing semantic meaning, structured relationships, and a faithful record of how facts change over time. It addresses the engineering challenges of keeping a company brain queryable in real-time while maintaining integrity.

- **Primary Goal**: Serving as the "brain-hosting" layer for an organization.
- **Differentiator**: Unlike static document stores, it manages the evolution of organizational truth, providing the structured relationships required for AI agent coordination.

## Relationships
- [[Company Brain]]: The higher-level organization-wide outcome supported by (and hosted within) HydraDB.
