# Explicit Content Flagging

The **Explicit Content Flagging** solution is one of the available [[oci-ai-accelerator-packs]] designed for content moderation and governance.

## Core Capabilities
- **Multimodal AI**: Leverages computer vision and potentially other models to analyze video assets.
- **Moderation Coverage**: Identifies and flags sensitive or inappropriate content automatically to ensure regulatory adherence and safety.
- **Business Outcome**: Automates compliance workflows that would otherwise be manually intensive.
