# Feedback Loop

A feedback loop turns human corrections into future system behavior.
In a company brain, each correction becomes a rule, preference, or routing update.

## Why it matters
- Converts ad hoc fixes into compounding operational memory.
- Keeps voice, source selection, and workflow routing improving over time.
- Prevents agents from repeating the same errors indefinitely.
