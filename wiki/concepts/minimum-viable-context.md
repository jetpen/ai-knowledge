# Minimum Viable Context

Curated organizational context that seeds a long-horizon AI strategy with only the judgments that are uniquely the company’s.

## Definition (from the X article)
Seed the system with the judgment only your company has, not everything it has ever said. Do not pull the whole company into a shared knowledge graph; curate it down in real time.

## What goes in
Start with decision points where human capital creates differentiated judgment, e.g.:
- strategy
- product tradeoffs
- customer understanding
- risk assessment
- market interpretation
- sales judgment
- engineering architecture
- operational playbooks

## What to avoid
- importing the entire company’s historical corpus into a shared “company brain”
- burying organizational learning inside opaque prompts/chats/misc individual memories

## Relation to long-horizon execution
Used as the shared foundation that makes work **structured**, **multiplayer**, and **evaluatable** over time (enabling learning loops and compounding rather than decay).
