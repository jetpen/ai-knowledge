## Team Size and Product Velocity

In the shift to AI in enterprise software, development teams are asked to enable agentic capabilities. The belief is that AI coding assistants accelerate development. But the pace of development remains glacial. Management doesn't understand why, if coding is accelerated by AI.

The answer is people. Even if coding effort and time is reduced to zero, the SDLC is not compressed much. The problem that is NOT solved by AI acceleration is establishing every human's understanding of the business problem. Then, so achieve understanding and consensus around the solution approach, so that implementation and testing can be done. Human communications complexity scales as O(n²) and does not reduce with AI enhancement. The only way to improve is to reduce n (human team members).
