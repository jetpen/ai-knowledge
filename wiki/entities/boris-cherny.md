# Boris Cherny

Boris Cherny is a software engineer at [[entities/anthropic.md|Anthropic]] and the lead creator/engineer behind [[entities/claude-code.md|Claude Code]].

## Perspective on Agentic Loops

Cherny has stated that he rarely prompts models directly anymore; instead, his workflow relies on building system-level loops that prompt the models iteratively for him. In this paradigm, the developer's core responsibility shifts from prompt design to designing and optimizing the execution/control loops around the model itself.

This shift forms a core dynamic of [[concepts/agent-loop-control-system.md|Agent Loop as Control System]].
