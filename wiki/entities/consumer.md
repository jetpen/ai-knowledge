# Consumer

**Agentic Coding Pattern** from [[agentic-coding-patterns]] (AiPatternBook REPL).

## Context
The client or dependent side of an [[interface]] or [[contract]]. Consumers invoke providers without knowing internals, enabling loose [[coupling]] and independent evolution.

## When to Use
- Defining APIs ([[api]])
- Microservices/multi-agent boundaries
- Plugin systems
