# Enterprise AI

## Overview/Sources
Enterprise AI platforms for secure, scalable agentic deployments: OCI, Azure AI, Bedrock Agents.

## Description
Enterprise AI shifts agents from prototypes to mission-critical ops with governance, compliance, SLAs. Platforms provide managed LLMs, vector stores, orchestration (LangGraph/CrewAI), monitoring. Agentic focus: Role-based access, audit trails, RAG over private data, multi-modal. OCI GenAI Agents exemplify: Accelerator packs for chat/search, integrated with ERP/CRM. Challenges: Hallucination guards, cost attribution, human oversight. Protocols MCP/A2A ensure interoperability across vendors. ROI: 30-50% efficiency in IT, sales, legal. Future: Autonomous orgs via agent swarms. (92 words)
