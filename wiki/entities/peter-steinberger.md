# Peter Steinberger

Peter Steinberger is an open-source advocate, developer, and the creator of [[entities/openclaw.md|OpenClaw]] (and the [[entities/openclaw-repository.md|OpenClaw Repository]]), an early local-first agent framework focused on proactive automation.

## Shift to Loop Engineering

Steinberger advocates for a direct transition away from manually prompting coding agents toward designing systematic execution loops that prompt models programmatically. He emphasizes that the system-level loop design, rather than model prompting, is the primary locus of control and reliability in agentic systems.
