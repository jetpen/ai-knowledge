# AMI Labs

## Overview
AMI Labs is an AI research ventures co-founded by **Yann LeCun** focused on the development of "world models"—AI systems that learn from reality rather than solely from massive corpus of language data.

## Key Facts
- **Funding:** Raised $1.03 billion at a $3.5 billion pre-money valuation.
- **Mission:** Building AI that understands the physical world to overcome the limitations and hallucinations of current LLMs.
- **Base Technology:** Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture (JEPA), an architecture pioneered by Yann LeCun.
- **Operational Approach:** Prioritizes fundamental research over rapid commercialization; strong commitment to open research and ecosystem building.

## Leadership
- **Chairman:** Yann LeCun
- **CEO:** Alexandre LeBrun
- **COO:** Laurent Solly
- **Chief Science Officer:** Saining Xie
- **Chief Research & Innovation Officer:** Pascale Fung
- **VP of World Models:** Michael Rabbat

## Funding & Backers
- **Investors:** Cathay Innovation, Greycroft, Hiro Capital, HV Capital, Bezos Expeditions, Nvidia, Samsung, Toyota Ventures.

## Operational Strategy
- **Headquarters:** Paris, with global hubs in New York, Montreal, and Singapore.
- **Resource Focus:** Compute infrastructure and talent acquisition.
