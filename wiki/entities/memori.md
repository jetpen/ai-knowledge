# Memori
Memori is an Italian company (founded in 2017) specializing in accessible, secure, and European-compliant Generative AI services. They provide a comprehensive ecosystem for enterprise AI adoption, ranging from team training to the development and deployment of autonomous AI Agents.

## Core Solutions
- **AIsuru**: A flagship European platform for creating, instructing, and deploying AI Agents in a unified environment. Supports deployment in cloud, private cloud, or on-premise configurations to meet data privacy and regulatory needs.
- **AIsuru AI Academy**: A professional training arm providing basic to advanced courses on Generative AI and AI platform usage, including mandatory technical training related to the European AI Act.
- **LLM Kit**: A tangible educational kit designed to make the mechanical workings of Generative AI accessible to non-technical users, trainers, and educators.

## Key Differentiators
- **Compliance-First**: Heavily focused on compliance with European and Italian regulations, including the European AI Act.
- **Enterprise-Grade Flexibility**: Offers end-to-end support from initial Proof of Concepts (PoCs) through to secure on-premise execution, leveraging frontier models (e.g., GPT, Claude) while ensuring data sovereignty.
- **Human-Centric Approach**: Philosophy centers on maintaining "humans at the center of technology," emphasizing team training alongside tool deployment.
