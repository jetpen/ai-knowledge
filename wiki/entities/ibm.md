# IBM Research

IBM Research is a global organization driving fundamental research and innovation in AI, hybrid cloud, and quantum computing. It is a key contributor to the development of open-source agentic ecosystems, including its partnership with Google Cloud on the **Agent2Agent (A2A) Protocol**.

## Key Initiatives
- **Agent2Agent (A2A) Protocol**: IBM Research partnered with Google Cloud to develop A2A, an open protocol that enables interoperability and communication between autonomous agents built on diverse frameworks.
- **DeepLearning.AI Collaboration**: Contributed to the development of the "A2A: The Agent2Agent Protocol" course, designed to educate developers on building A2A-compliant agents, orchestrating multi-agent systems (e.g., in healthcare), and understanding the relationship between A2A and the Model Context Protocol (MCP).
- **Framework Support**: Through initiatives like BeeAI, IBM Research emphasizes the importance of framework-agnostic communication standards to break down AI silos and enable complex, multi-agent collaboration without sacrificing security or proprietary intellectual property.
