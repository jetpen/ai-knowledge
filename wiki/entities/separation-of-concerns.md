## Agentic Application
Separation of Concerns (SoC) isolates agent functions: planning logic separate from execution, memory from tools. Classes: Planner, Executor, MemoryManager. Reduces coupling, eases testing/mocks. LLM prompts per concern. Evolves monoliths. With [[cohesion]]. (92 words)
