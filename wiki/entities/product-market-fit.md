## Agentic Application
Product-Market Fit (PMF) confirms agent solves real pains for paying customers at scale. Metrics: retention >40%, referral rates, LTV>3x CAC, Net Promoter Score >50. For agents: users re-run daily, expand usage. Test via landing pages, beta cohorts tracking activation (first successful task). Iterate prompts/tools based on failure modes. Agents self-assess PMF via user feedback loops. Pitfalls: vanity metrics; focus sequential growth stages. Leads to [[crossing-the-chasm]]. (132 words)
