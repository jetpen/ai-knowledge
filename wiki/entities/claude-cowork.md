# Claude Cowork

## Overview
Claude Cowork is an [[anthropic]] product designed for delegating knowledge work, featuring scheduled automation, multi-platform integrations (Slack, Notion, GitHub, etc.), and human-in-the-loop controls.

## Capabilities
- **Task Delegation**: Hand off tasks to get polished deliverables.
- **Cross-Platform**: Integrates directly with standard enterprise tools like Slack, Notion, and Linear.
- **Automation**: Supports scheduled tasks for recurring workflows (daily briefings, etc.).
- **Control**: Includes user-defined access permissions and mandatory human approval for significant actions.
